What are oral probiotics?

Oral probiotics are supplements containing selected microorganisms intended to complement an oral-care routine. Evidence, strains, quantities, and product quality can differ. Buyers should inspect the exact formula and discuss suitability with a professional when appropriate.

Questions to ask before buying

  • Are the ingredients and amounts clearly listed?
  • Does the seller avoid unrealistic or guaranteed claims?
  • Are pricing, shipping, contact information, and refund terms clear?
  • Could an ingredient conflict with medication, allergies, pregnancy, or an existing condition?

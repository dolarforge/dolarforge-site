EDITOR'S SUMMARY

Bottom line

LeanBiome combines a microbiome story with probiotic strains, prebiotic support, and plant compounds used in weight-management supplements. The offer is commercially established, but probiotic effects are strain-specific and the evidence for meaningful weight loss remains mixed. Any expected effect should be considered modest and secondary to nutrition, activity, sleep, and medical factors.
